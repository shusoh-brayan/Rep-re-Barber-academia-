Our Story

More Than a Barbershop

Repère Barber Academia was built on a simple principle: every man deserves to look and feel his best. Situated at Carrefour Black Power, Grand Hangar — right beside Pafass University — we are Douala's premium destination for precision grooming and genuine relaxation.

The word Repère means "landmark" in French. That's exactly what we intend to be — a landmark of quality, consistency, and craftsmanship in a city that deserves nothing less. Whether you need a clean fade, a shaped beard, a head massage, or a pedicure, our trained barbers deliver with attention to every detail.
